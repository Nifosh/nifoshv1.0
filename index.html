About usWhat exactly NIFOSH is?

NIFOSH is one of the best institutions in the field of fire Engineering and Safety Management. NIFOSH is conducting various courses in Diploma, Advanced Diploma and Post Graduate Diploma programs in the field of Industrial / General Safety, Fire Engineering, Health and Environment Safety and Disaster Management. The courses offered by We provide the effective and value based Training of safety in the field of Construction, Infrastructure, Complex Industrial Hazards, etc. The Institute also seeks to comply with its practical training programmers for the ongoing students so that they can establish themselves in the industry with a handsome package.   The aim of the institution is not only to provide high quality professional education to the students but it is also giving a good stuff of youth to the society. It is because of the aim of the institution it has chosen the field of fire and safety to work in. By remaining in the field of Fire and Safety the institution has the following objectives:
1. To minimize the loss of life and property by producing skilled professionals in the field.
2. To give better job opportunities to unemployed youths.
3. To give a sincere contribution in the new developments of the field.
4. To create general awareness in the field of fire and safety.
